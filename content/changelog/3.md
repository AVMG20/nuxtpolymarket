title: Live Chat
description: Talk to other players in real time, from any page.
date: 2026-07-07
---

There's now a chat widget in the bottom-right corner of every page.

- **Real time** — messages arrive instantly over WebSockets. A badge counts unread messages while the chat is closed.
- **Formatting** — bold, underline and strikethrough. Enter sends, Shift+Enter adds a line break.
- **Share your stats** — one-click buttons drop your coin balance, gem count, or today's best win / worst loss straight into the message.
- **History** — the last 20 messages load on open; scroll up for more.
- **Your messages, your rules** — delete anything you've sent.
