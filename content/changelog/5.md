title: Hybrid Fixes
description: Hybrids now grow as fast as their slowest plant, and always cost a sensible minimum in gems.
date: 2026-07-08
---

Two fixes to hybrid plants in Xeno. Hackops changes

- **Parallel growth** — a hybrid's grow time is now the max of its resources' speed-adjusted grow times, not the sum. A 4-plant hybrid grows as fast as its slowest component, instead of stacking every resource's time on top of each other.
- **Flat gem price** — hybrid rolls now cost a fixed amount of gems based on tier, no longer tied to the live gem market: 4 gems at T4, +2 gems per tier above that (T5 = 6, T6 = 8, …).
- **Artifact crafting tooltips** — hovering a plant icon in the Artifacts crafting cost list now shows the same plant tooltip (stats, grow time, value) used in the Market, making it easier to identify which plant you need.

## Hackops changes
Introduces a gem-based item leveling system at the new Crafting Bench,
allowing items to gain +2 power per level up to a max of 20.
Crate and agent recruit tiers now guarantee a minimum rarity by removing
lower rarity options, clarifying that higher tiers provide more mods
(rarity), not better individual mod roll quality.

All item mods now roll from their full potential range, regardless of source.
Item sell prices are now fixed per rarity, decoupling them from item level.

## Fire in the Hole rebalance

Reworked the RTP split and added a new base-game mechanic.

- **New coin drop** — roughly 1 in 3 base-game spins, a coin lands on top of a tile. It doesn't join cascade matches itself, but if a bomb blows up its tile, the coin pops and adds a flat multiplier of your bet directly to that win.
- **RTP rebalance** — base game now pays out ~35-40% of total RTP (up from ~19%), bonus free spins ~60-70% (down from ~90%), for a total RTP around 97-98.5%.
- **More frequent bonus** — free spins now trigger about 1 in 100 spins, up from roughly 1 in 175.
- **Buy bonus price adjusted** to match the new bonus payout curve.
- Symbol pay values and bonus coin/boost values retuned to keep the ~10,000x realistic max win intact while hitting the new RTP targets.
