---
title: Hybrid Grow Times Fixed
description: Hybrids now grow as fast as their slowest plant, not the sum of all of them.
date: 2026-07-08
---

Hybrid plants in Xeno grew painfully slowly, since their grow time stacked every resource's time on top of each other.

- **Parallel growth** — a hybrid's grow time is now the max of its resources' speed-adjusted grow times, not the sum. A 4-plant hybrid grows as fast as its slowest component.
