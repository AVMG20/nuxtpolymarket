---
title: Gem Exchange
description: The Gem Market is now a player-driven Grand Exchange with buy and sell offers.
date: 2026-07-21
---

- **Gem Exchange** — the old auto-scaling Gem Market is gone. Gems now trade on a player-driven exchange: place buy or sell offers at any price, and they match against other players' offers best-price-first. Offer more than a seller asks and you pay their lower price — the difference comes straight back, Grand Exchange style.
- **Escrowed offers** — buy offers escrow your coins and sell offers escrow your gems until they fill or you cancel, so every open offer is always fully backed.
- **Live everything** — the order book, live offers and recent trades update in real time over a websocket, with the guide price computed from actual trades (24h volume-weighted average).
- **Smart pricing** — the offer form defaults to the price that trades instantly (cheapest sell offer when buying, highest buy offer when selling) with quick ±5% / ±20% nudge buttons.
- **Leaderboard** — gem wealth is now valued at the exchange guide price, and coins or gems locked in open offers still count toward your total wealth.
