---
title: Economy, Trading & Combat Updates
description: Faster gem trading, cash-only lootboxes, fairer leaderboards, and new gem-powered cooldown skips.
date: 2026-07-22
---

- **Trade straight from the order book.** Click any Gem Exchange bid or ask to prefill a sell or buy order that trades through that price level. The form scrolls into view automatically, and recent trades now clearly show who bought from whom.
- **Lootboxes are now cash-only.** Every prize is a vault-scaled cash reward, including paid opens; Rig Overclock boosts all payouts. The prize pool and opening flow have been simplified to match.
- **Fairer Xeno leaderboard totals.** Duplicate plant-unlock records can no longer be created, and existing duplicates no longer inflate a player's unlocked-species count.
- **Spend gems to skip recovery time.** Pirate ship repairs and Shapezz Arena cooldowns can now be rushed for one gem per started ten minutes remaining.
- **Shapezz combat rebalance.** Nova Mortar has a broader blast with strong core damage that falls off toward the edge, while Scatter Array damage and visual intensity have been reduced. The cash-out offer now uses the same payout ceiling as the server, so the displayed reward matches what is awarded.
