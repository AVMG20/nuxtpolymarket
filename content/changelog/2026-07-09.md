---
title: Leaderboard Fixes
description: Xeno and Hack Ops leaderboards now match their in-game pages.
date: 2026-07-09
---

- **Xeno species unlocked fix** — the Xeno leaderboard now counts species unlocked from the permanent unlock record instead of currently-owned plants, so selling or breeding away a plant no longer lowers your species count. It also excludes hybrids from the count, matching the Xenopedia's discovered total.
- **Hack Ops power fix** — the Hack Ops leaderboard now only counts active (rostered) agents toward total power and agent count, matching the `/hack` page. Stored/inactive agents no longer inflate your leaderboard power.
- **Blackjack bet cap removed** — the max bet limit on Blackjack has been removed, and bet/payout amounts now use compact number formatting to support larger bets.
- **Garden artifact swapping** — you can now drop a selected artifact onto a grid slot that already has one; it replaces the old artifact and returns it straight to your inventory. Slots that would be replaced are clearly marked with an amber ring and a swap icon.
- **Inventory cleanup** — selecting an artifact in the Garden or Breeder inventory now shows a Delete button for clearing out junk stacks. It requires a second confirming click and gives no refund.
- **Inventory sorting** — Seeds and Artifacts tabs now have a sort dropdown (tier, quantity, speed, yield, level, mutation, etc.) with a direction toggle. Your sort preference is remembered per tab.
