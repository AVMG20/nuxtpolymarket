We've added a new slot to the lineup: [Book of Shadows](/games/bookofshadows).

Book of Shadows is a volatile game with max wins between **5,000x and 10,000x** your bet. Unlike most volatile slots, the base game is worth sticking around for too — it pays out **35-40%** of the total RTP on its own, with the bonus round contributing the remaining **60-70%**.

This release also comes with a full set of added assets, plus sound effects and background music to bring the game to life.

Good luck out there.
