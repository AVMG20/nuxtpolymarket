---
title: Xeno Endgame Expansion
description: New global upgrades, two plant tiers, and a stronger artifact endgame.
date: 2026-07-20
---

- **Global Xeno upgrades.** Invest in permanent mutation, yield, and cultivation speed bonuses from the Market, with expensive endgame levels to keep progression meaningful.
- **Two new plant tiers.** T8 and T9 plants extend the mutation chain and open up more valuable late-game harvests.
- **Expanded artifacts.** New top-tier artifacts use T8 and T9 plants, older breeder artifacts grant an extra mutation level, and the crafting list is now grouped by family.
