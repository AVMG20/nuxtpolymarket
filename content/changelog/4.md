description: Click a category to filter your stats and chart down to just that game.

---

The analytics page now lets you drill into a single category.

- **Category filter** — click a category (or a transaction's category) to filter the stats cards, performance chart, and transaction list down to just that one.
- **Clear anytime** — a banner and clear buttons let you jump back to the full view.
