---
title: Economy, Shapezz, and Pirate Raid Updates
description: Colony and Xeno progression have been rebalanced, Shapezz has new combat content and a stronger reward curve, and Pirate Raid now rewards longer and completed voyages.
date: 2026-07-23
---

- **Colony resource sales** — all foraged item sell values are down 15%, reducing the coin income from late-game colony setups.
- **Habitat upgrades** — colony resource requirements now start at 5× their previous amounts and scale to 10× for higher track levels, making stockpiles and ongoing production matter throughout progression.
- **Xeno plant sales** — every Xeno plant is worth more. T1–T3 plants receive a 15% increase, with smaller increases at higher tiers (12% at T4 down to 3% at T9) to avoid runaway endgame income.
- **Shapezz weaponry** — added the Arc Coil, a chain-lightning weapon with upgradeable range and chain jumps.
- **Shapezz run upgrades** — added Killquake, Executioner, Overkill Dividend, and Ceiling Battery, with new effects for kill streaks, executions, excess damage, and an arena turret.
- **Shapezz balance and aiming** — enemies now drop coins whose rewards grow more gradually across later rounds, Ceiling Battery turrets copy your upgraded weapon volleys without triggering healing effects, and runs have a visible mouse aiming reticle.
- **Pirate Raid rewards** — reduced overall income slightly, moved most high-power difficulty rewards into the later voyage, and greatly increased the six-minute completion bonus. Starting an oversized voyage and surviving only a minute or two is no longer an effective farming strategy.
- **Pirate Raid audio** — leaving the raid now fully stops sea ambience and prevents delayed seagull sounds from playing on unrelated pages.
