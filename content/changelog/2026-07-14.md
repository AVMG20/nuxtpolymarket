Pirate Raid gets an economy and survivability pass, and there's a brand-new idle game to try.

## Pirate Raid

- **Bigger payouts.** Voyages pay ~2.5× more loot, and finishing the full 8 minutes now adds a **completion bonus** on top — a completed run earns roughly 3.5× the old rate. The bonus is shown per difficulty when you set sail.
- **New upgrade: Life Regen.** Passively repairs your hull (+1/s, up to +5/s) once you've been **out of combat for 6 seconds** — taking a hit or firing resets it.
- **Power-ups last ~30% longer** across the board.
- **Blood Tide** now heals a flat +1 hull per hit instead of scaling with damage.
- **Cannons cost more** (the main progression sink) and ship-stat upgrades are slightly pricier, to match the richer economy.

## New game: Colony 🐛

Breed and raise a colony of alien bugs, place them in your habitat to earn resources while you're away, keep them fed, and research rarer species to sell for more. It's our first proper idle game — play it at **/colony**.
