---
title: Hack Ops Redesign
description: A full visual and audio overhaul of Hack Ops — new tabs, cinematics, voice lines, and agent portraits.
date: 2026-07-12
---

Hack Ops has had a full presentation overhaul — same missions, agents, and gear underneath, but almost every screen looks and sounds different.

## New tabs

- **Black Market** — a new hub for recruiting agents and buying gear crates, replacing the old Recruit/Crates sections on the Agents and Items pages. Recruiting and crate-opening now play out as full reveal cinematics with rarity stamps, instead of a plain result popup.
- **Loadout** — a dedicated equip screen: roster on one side, gear bays in the middle, inventory on the other. Includes a before/after comparison overlay so you can see exactly what a gear swap changes before confirming, and supports drag-and-drop as well as click-to-equip.

## Redesigned screens

- **Ops** — mission briefings now play out as an in-page cinematic with a scrolling RELAY dossier voiceover, instead of a plain dispatch modal. Mission collection got the same reveal-cinematic treatment.
- **Agents** — roster and sleeper-agent cards redesigned, now showing power, gear, and rarity-tinted portraits at a glance. Narrowed to roster management now that equipping lives on the new Loadout page.
- **Items** — crafting bench redesigned with a clearer upgrade flow (single/bulk level buttons, gem cost preview, lock toggles for re-rolling), and inventory cards show rarity-colored mods.
- **History** — past ops now show as a debrief log, with reward chips and an expandable RELAY one-liner per entry.
- **Leaderboard** — Most Wanted now shows the top 3 as a podium with larger dossier cards.

## Voice & sound

- Mission briefings, market pitches, recruit/crate reveals, and roster actions (activate, fire, equip) now have RELAY voice lines and captions, with rarity-based barks on rare pulls.
- New SFX for clicks, purchases, deploys, and denied actions throughout Hack Ops.
- Voice lines are throttled so they don't talk over each other or fire on every single click, and correctly stop when you navigate away or skip ahead.

## Agent portraits & names

- **New agent codenames** — agents now draw from class-themed name pools (stealthy names for Infiltrators, cryptic ones for Cryptographers, and so on) instead of one shared list, so rosters feel less repetitive.
- **New agent portraits** — agents now show a tinted sigil icon instead of a shared class photo, colored by rarity. Elite and Phantom agents draw from a fancier icon set.

## Fixes

- Mission briefing audio no longer gets talked over by the squad-select voice line — picking your squad while the briefing is still playing now waits until it finishes.
- Fixed a bug where leaving a briefing or market pitch mid-line could leave its voice clip playing in the background.
- Fixed item mod bonus ordering and a reward-roll clamp so both match the actual in-game math.
