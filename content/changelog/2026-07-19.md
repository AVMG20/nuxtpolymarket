---
title: Hack Ops Rebalance
description: Bigger endgame payouts, faster XP on hard ops, and per-rarity power caps that make rarity matter.
date: 2026-07-19
---

- **The climb finally pays.** Endgame operations (NSA Breach through Project Zero) now pay out much more, with reward-per-hour that keeps rising as you invest in power — instead of flatlining at the top.
- **Hard ops level your deck fast.** Higher-tier ops now grant far more XP, so running tough missions is the quickest way to level your agents.
- **Rarity now has a power ceiling.** Every agent is capped by rarity — Ghost 200, Operative 300, Specialist 400, Elite 500, Phantom 600 — no matter how it's leveled or geared. Tougher ops genuinely require higher-rarity agents: only Phantoms can reliably run Quantum Heist, and only Phantoms can seriously attempt Project Zero. The cap is shown on every agent card and explained in the Wiki.
- **Shorter top-tier ops.** Ghost Protocol, Quantum Heist and Project Zero take a little less time to complete.
