---
title: Leaderboard Fix
description: Xeno leaderboard now tracks species unlocked correctly.
date: 2026-07-09
---

- **Species unlocked fix** — the Xeno leaderboard now counts species unlocked from the permanent unlock record instead of currently-owned plants, so selling or breeding away a plant no longer lowers your species count. It also now excludes hybrids from the count, matching the Xenopedia's discovered total.
