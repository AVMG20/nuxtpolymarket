---
title: Leaderboard Fix
description: Xeno leaderboard now tracks species unlocked correctly.
date: 2026-07-09
---

- **Species unlocked fix** — the Xeno leaderboard now counts species unlocked from the permanent unlock record instead of currently-owned plants, so selling or breeding away a plant no longer lowers your species count.
- **Blackjack bet cap removed** — the max bet limit on Blackjack has been removed, and bet/payout amounts now use compact number formatting to support larger bets.
